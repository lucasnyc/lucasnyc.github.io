A little backstory from 2019 to 2024, I was a finance professional with two of the largest bank in Singapore as a Relationship Manager. It was a wealth management role for high net worth clients in the aspect of insurance, investment and treasury products. While I had been consistently ranked in the 90th percentile in revenue generation for the bank, I no longer had the passion and contentment from the achirvements at work. Along with Covid in 2020, many thoughts had crossed my
mind. One that sticked on was, is this job the only thing I will be doing and be good at for the rest of my maybe decades of upcoming career?
